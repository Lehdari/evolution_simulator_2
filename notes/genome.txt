Creature Genome:

0:  Creature size (once adulthood has been reached)
1:  Creature metabolic constant (1: 100% from plants, 0% from meat, 0: vice versa)
2:  Minimum child size (relative)
3:  Maximum child size (relative)
4:  Child energy (0: 0, 1: maximum(mass*massEnergyStorageConstant))
5:  Color, Red channel
6:  Color, Green channel
7:  Color, Blue Channel
8: Mutation probability, 1st pass
9: Mutation amplitude, 1st pass
10: Mutation probability, 2nd pass
11: Mutation amplitude, 2nd pass
